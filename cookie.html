Cookie Policy

At Navira Life Essentials Pvt. Ltd., we value transparency and your right to privacy. This Cookie Policy explains how and why we use cookies when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Quick summary

We use cookies to improve site experience, analyze traffic, personalize content, and enable features like social sharing. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

  • Session Cookies (temporary)
  • Persistent Cookies (remember preferences)
  • Third-party analytics (Google Analytics)

Full Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, smartphone, or tablet when you visit a website. They usually contain information such as the website name, duration, and a unique identifier.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience and website performance
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior for improvement
  • Personalize content and user preferences
  • Enable features like social sharing and interactive tools

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies – These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies – These remain on your device for a set duration to remember preferences and settings.

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable or manage cookies through your browser settings. However, some sections of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and user behavior. This data helps us improve usability without collecting any personally identifiable information. You can read Google’s Privacy Policy at www.google.com/privacy_policy.html.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may periodically update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
